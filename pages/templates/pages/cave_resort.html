Ownership & Operations

How cottage ownership works

A full cottage — land, structure, and 16 trees — is offered at approximately ₹1.5 crore, with title and ownership documents transferred to the buyer. Day-to-day marketing, bookings, and financial administration of the resort are handled by the company, with regular reporting to owners.

For those not looking to buy a full cottage outright, smaller entry points are available through our Group Farming and Farm Forest projects, or by purchasing resort access/stay passes once the property opens.

On rental income: Day-rate and occupancy figures sometimes quoted for this resort are forward estimates for a property still under construction, not historical performance. Actual rental income will depend on completed amenities, demand, seasonality, and operating costs once the resort is open — ask for current, written projections before relying on any number.
