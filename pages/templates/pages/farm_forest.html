Sandalwood Farm Forest

A 12-acre integrated farm tourism and sandalwood research project — combining cultivation, agri-income streams, and visitor experiences.

The Concept

Integrated Farm Tourism & Sandalwood Research Centre

Six income streams running alongside sandalwood cultivation, plus Kerala's first dedicated sandalwood research facility.

Cattle & Poultry

Cattle rearing and a large-scale chicken farm form two of the six planned income streams on site.

Duck & Fish Farming

Integrated duck and fish farming designed to work alongside the existing cultivation plan.

Vegetables, Marigold & Ginger

Vegetable cultivation alongside marigold and ginger crops, already underway on parts of the site.

Poultry Share Programme

A share-based entry point into the chicken farm

The chicken farm component is structured as a share programme: 10,000 shares at ₹1 lakh each, tied to a planned operation of roughly one lakh birds.

  • ✓ Entry per share: ₹1,00,000
  • ✓ Planned scale: ~1,00,000 birds
  • ✓ Income source: egg production
On projected returns: Per-share egg revenue figures quoted for this programme are forward projections based on planned scale, not a guaranteed payout. Actual returns depend on flock health, market egg prices, and operating costs. Ask for the share agreement and audited terms before investing.
